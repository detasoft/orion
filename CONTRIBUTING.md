# Contributing

Orion accepts contributions that can be included in the project under the
license model described in `LICENSE`.

## Contributor License Agreement

Before submitting code, documentation, tests, examples, or other copyrightable
material, you must agree to the Orion Contributor License Agreement in `CLA.md`.

For individual contributions, add this statement to the pull request:

```text
I agree to the Orion Contributor License Agreement v1.0.
```

If the contribution is made as part of your employment or on behalf of an
organization, the organization must have the rights needed to submit the
contribution and DETA PRO B.V. may require a corporate CLA before accepting it.

CLA and contribution licensing questions can be sent to info@deta-it.com or
info@detasoft.net.

## Contribution Rules

- Submit only work that you wrote or are authorized to contribute.
- Keep third-party code out of contributions unless its license is clearly
  compatible and disclosed in the pull request.
- Preserve existing copyright, license, and notice headers.
- Do not use Orion, DETA, or DETA PRO branding for forks or derivative services
  in ways that imply endorsement. See `TRADEMARKS.md`.

## License of Contributions

Accepted contributions are included in Orion under the project license in
`LICENSE`. Contributors keep copyright in their contributions, but grant
DETA PRO B.V. the rights described in `CLA.md`.

The CLA is required so DETA PRO B.V. can keep the ability to change Orion's
licensing model, offer commercial licenses, dual-license the project, or
relicense accepted contributions as part of Orion without asking each
contributor for additional permission later.
